Demand utilization

For each route in My Routes, compare daily seats you offer (configuration × trips × aircraft assigned) to extracted demand (Y/J/F). Each cabin is classified as Underserved (unmet demand), Saturated (roughly balanced, within −10% of demand), or Wasted (capacity above demand by more than 10%).

Note: AM4 demand replenishes in cycles, so persistent unmet demand on routes you fly is a stronger signal than a single snapshot. Use this view as a guide, not ground truth for revenue left behind.
